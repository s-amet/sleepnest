Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17.02.2025

1. Overview

Sleepnest values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Sleepnest application.

2. Data Collected

Sleepnest is designed to be a privacy-friendly, distraction-free experience.
We do not collect or store any personal data.

The app may locally store the following information only on your device:

Selected ambient sounds and mixes

Sleep timer settings

Breathing or relaxation preferences

App appearance preferences (such as theme or visual mode)

3. Use of Data

Any locally stored data is used solely to:

Provide sound playback and sleep features

Remember your preferences

Improve the in-app user experience

Sleepnest does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or tracking.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data:

Is stored locally on your device

Is never transmitted to external servers

Is not shared with third parties

Is protected by the operating system’s security mechanisms

Sleepnest does not use cloud storage, analytics tracking, or third-party data collection services.

5. User Rights

As a user, you have full control over your data:

You can reset app settings at any time

All locally stored data is deleted when the app is removed from your device

6. Data Retention

All data remains on your device only

No backups are created by Sleepnest

Deleting the app permanently removes all associated data

7. Children’s Privacy

Sleepnest does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when necessary. Any significant changes will be reflected within the app or on the App Store listing.
